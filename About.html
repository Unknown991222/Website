About me

Hello there, Allow me to introduce myself - I'm Rochelle! I wanted to take a moment to share a brief glimpse into the person behind the camera. At 27 years old, I am finally pursuing my long-held aspiration of becoming a photographer. It took me a while to muster the courage, possibly because I identify as an introvert. However, my deep love for nature and a free-spirited mindset have propelled me to take this leap of faith.


When I set out to find the perfect photo that truly represents who I am, I had the great fortune of working with NALA Photography, an incredibly talented photographer. Together, we captured the essence of a country girl confined within the soul of a city dweller. If I had one wish, it would be to live a life adorned with cowboy boots and the ability to perform an incredible line dance. Sporting bright purple hair and embarking on this journey as a photographer, I strive to immortalize life's precious moments with an artistic touch


While I am still honing my skills in the realm of photography, my goal is to steadily improve, one shoot at a time. I aspire to become one of the best in the field. Until we meet again,
Rochelle
